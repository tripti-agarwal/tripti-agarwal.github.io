    Hello!

    I am a first year PhD student at University of Utah, advised by the fantastic faculty Dr. Ganesh Gopalakrishnan.

    My research centers on precision tuning and building new algorithms for different scientific datasets. I am also interested in working on GPUs and building GPU based algorithm for scientific computing. Some recent interests include concurrency and precision tuning from the lens of topological data analysis.

    My previous reserach was based on Topological Data Analysis for multi-field data. I have worked in this field for 3 years with Dr. Amit Chattopadhyay. We even collaborated with Dr. Vijay Natarajan in one of our projects.

    My recent interest are in learning how precision tuning and compression techinques used for large datasets and big scientific compuatation and building new algorithms and tools for the same.
